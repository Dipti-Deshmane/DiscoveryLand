The Discovery Story

For almost three decades, Discovery has built communities that encourage
both our development teams and members to dream up and create the most
fun experiences imaginable. The first development in Scottsdale set the
standard for a Discovery community, complete with a championship golf
course, comfort stations brimming with snacks, luxurious residential offerings,
five-star service, and more. Since then, each community has built on the fun
from the one that came before.
